Plantsfinder is a plant catalog that automatically curates a selection of plants. It caters to both amateur gardeners and professionals in the field of landscape design. The content is presented in simple language that is easily understandable even for individuals without specialized knowledge. However, the plant descriptions are comprehensive, providing all the necessary information for gardening work.

Plantsfinder proves helpful in the following situations:

  1. When you find it challenging to decide which plants to cultivate, you can input specific search parameters such as frost resistance zone, humidity, illumination, and more. This generates a list of plants that match the characteristics of your site.
  2. If you have a particular plant in mind but want to double-check its suitability for your area, Plantsfinder allows you to verify your selection. By doing so, you can avoid unsuccessful experiments with plants that may not be suitable for your specific location, saving both time and money.
  3. If you have already planted certain plants but they are not thriving, the issue may not be related to diseases or pests, but rather their placement. Understanding the specific needs of the plant, you can take appropriate action, such as relocating it to a more suitable spot or adjusting care practices like soil acidity, watering frequency, and more.

The selection criteria for plants are organized in order of importance, with the most critical factors listed first. You begin by specifying the characteristics of your planting area, followed by the desired visual aspects of the plant, including width, height, decorative features, and other relevant details.
